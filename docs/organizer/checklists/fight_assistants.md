# Fight Assistant Checklist

## 1. Pick up the papers at the office
(20 minutes before fight)

- 15-20 **score sheets**
- 1 **overview sheet**
- 4 **preview sheets** (from round 2 onwards)
- 4 **Physics Fight Guidelines**
- 1 **Chairperson's Responsibilities**
- 1 **Regulations** of the IYPT
- **non-voting juror approval forms**
- **Team namesigns**
- **Juror namesigns**
- **USB sticks**: One per team

## 2. Room preparation before the round
- Distribute **team signs** to the team tables (1 per team).
- Distribute **USB sticks** to the team tables (1 per team).
- Distribute the **PF guidelines** to the team tables (1 per team).
- Distribute the **fight preview** sheets to the team tables (from round 2 onwards).
- Distribute **juror namesigns** on the jury tables in order of the **overview sheet**.
- Say hello to the chairperson of the fight and hand them the **Chair guidelines**, **PF guidelines** and **Regulations**.
- From round 2 onwards, hand the chairperson a **fight preview**.
- If there are **non-voting juror approval forms**, hand them to the chairperson.
- Distribute the **grading sheets** for the first stage to the jurors, one per juror.
    They have the name and the stage pre-filled.
- Log into the IYPT CC at [https://cc.iypt.org/](https://cc.iypt.org/)
    - On the first login, go to Profile -> Tournament, select IYPT 2025 as Active Tournament and click Change Tournament.
    - The menu on the left should now have a Fight -> Overview entry.
    - Select your room and the current round (only done by 1 FRA), they are highlighted in blue.
    - Check if the jury in the CC is the same order as the **juror namesigns** you distributed.
    - Go to Stage 1 and open Clock.
        - The clock opens in a new tab.
        - Detach the tab and drag it to the external display.
        - You can control the clock with the buttons on the control panel in the Stage area.

## 3. During the stages

- Start the clock when the chair advises you to do so.
- Fill the **overview sheet** with acting persons.
- Note the rejected problems and the accepted problem.
- At grading, write down the grades when read out by the chair:
    - once into the form in the CC
    - once on the **overview sheet**

## 4. After every stage

- Collect the **grading sheets** from jurors.
- Distribute new **grading sheets** for the next stage to jurors.
- Go to the next Stage with "Save and next Stage" in the CC.

## 5. After all stages at the end of the round

- Collect the **juror namesigns**.
- Collect the **team signs**.
- Collect the **USB sticks**.
- Check in the "Check" tab if the teams submitted feedback. If not, remind them to do so.
- "Save and Lock" the fight in CC.
- Make sure you collected everything that you distributed.
- Bring all documents and usb sticks to the office.
