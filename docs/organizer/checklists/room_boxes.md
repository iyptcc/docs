The boxes for rooms should contain the following printed documents

## Folded thick paper
- Juror name signs (5-8)
- Team origin signs (3-4)

## Grading

- Overview A3 sheet 1
- Grading sheets (15-20)
- Non-voting grading sheets (up to 12)
- Non-voting approval sheets (1 per non-voting)

## Problems
- Preview sheet (rounds 2-5)
- Problem selection sheet (rounds 4,5)
- USB keys (1 per team)

## Non-personalised

- [Regulations](https://www.iypt.org/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/IYPT-Regulations-2025.pdf)
- [Physics Fight guidelines](http://iypt.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/PF_guidelines_2017.pdf)
- [Chair guidelines](http://iypt.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/03/Chairpersons_ticklist_2017.pdf)
- [Fight Assistant checklist](fight_assistants.md)
- Grading booklet (1 per juror)
